Chronicles of the Furious Five

DRAGON

虎 猴 螳 蛇 鶴

Five masters. Five legends.
One scribe to remember them all.

begin the chronicle
I.

Tigress

The will of steel

She breaks stones with her stare.
She breaks armies with her silence.
Discipline, given a heartbeat.

II.

Monkey

The laughing fist

He fights with laughter,
and laughs with fists.
You will lose. You will smile losing.

III.

Mantis

The patient blade

Small enough to hide in a breath.
Sharp enough to halve the wind.
Wait. Wait. Strike.

IV.

Viper

The silent dance

No fangs. No fear.
Her dance is the dance of seasons —
and seasons always win.

V.

Crane

The dancing sky

He carries weight without weight.
He flies without leaving.
The wind owes him a favour.

The chronicle ends here. The legend does not.

Together — Five.
Apart — myth.
